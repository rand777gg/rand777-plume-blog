Each week I search web like *US News, Springer Nature, IEEE, web.dev* articles, and write a journal or summary in English. This is a good way to practice my English writing skills and learn new vocabulary, get to know the BIG in the world by the way.

For advance or uncommon words, sentences and expressions, I use ==underlines== to highlight them, give out the *[Oxford Dictionary](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/)* definition, annotate \[+annotate] and add external relative links. This helps me remember the context and usage of those.

\[+annotate]: You can click on the underlined words to see their definitions or explanations, may refer to [external links](/hobby/journal-weekly/begin/).
